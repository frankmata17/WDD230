Visual Hierarchy

Apple

apple.com

Apple likes to highlight their products using bold text and white backgrounds. In this example they are promoting their newest product, letting the consumer focus only on their product.

Rule of Thirds

Goal

goal.com

Goal creates visually stunning images to captivate their audience. In this home page example they use images of the same person to fill the main article's foreground and use an image of the stadium in the background.

Repetition

Best Buy


bestbuy.com

Best Buy's website is a great example of repetition style. Although the home page includes different types of products, they are all styled with circle border icons with images of the product inside the icons. This creates order and makes it easy for a consumer to find what they are looking for.
